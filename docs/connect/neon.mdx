---
title: Connect Neon
description: Connect Dora to a Neon serverless Postgres database with a connection string or your Neon account.
---

Neon is serverless PostgreSQL. Dora connects with a standard connection string
and enables SSL automatically for `*.neon.tech` hosts.

## With a connection string

1. In the Neon Console, open your project and click **Connect** (or
   **Connection Details** on the dashboard).
2. Copy the connection string. It looks like:

   ```text
   postgresql://<user>:<password>@<endpoint>.neon.tech/<db>?sslmode=require
   ```

3. In Dora press `Mod+Shift+N`, toggle **Use connection string**, paste the URL,
   **Test connection**, and **Save**.

<Callout type='info'>**Pooled vs direct.** Neon's pooled host contains `-pooler` in the endpoint. Use it for serverless/short-lived connections; use the non-pooler host for sessions that need a direct connection. Both work in Dora.</Callout>

## With your Neon account

Choose **Connect with Neon** in the connection dialog and sign in. Dora lists
your projects and **branches** and builds the connection string for the branch
you pick.

SSL is required and is set automatically.
